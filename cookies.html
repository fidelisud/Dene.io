Cookies Policy

Last Updated: October 6, 2025

1. Introduction

DANIELLO ENERGIA (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (“Website”) to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content related to our oil and gas solutions, such as offshore drilling, pipeline integrity, and seismic surveying. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track usage and improve functionality. We may also use similar technologies, such as pixels or web beacons, for similar purposes

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Website to function, such as enabling navigation and access to secure areas like enquiry forms
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how you use the Website, such as pages visited and time spent, to improve performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language settings or form inputs, to provide a personalized experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements or track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns (with your consent where required)

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the Website operates smoothly, such as when you submit an enquiry for our Services or products like drilling rig modules
  • Analyze Website performance and user behavior to optimize content and navigation
  • Personalize your experience, such as remembering your preferences for viewing service or product details.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts (if applicable)

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as analytics providers or advertising partners, that set their own cookies. For example, we may use Google Analytics to track Website usage. These third parties have their own privacy and cookies policies, which we encourage you to review

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings or by responding to our cookie consent popup (if enabled). You can:

  • Accept or decline non-essential cookies via our consent popup
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect Website functionality
  • Delete cookies from your device at any time through your browser

7. Cookies Consent

When you first visit our Website, you may see a cookies consent popup asking for your permission to use non-essential cookies. By accepting, you allow us to use cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date. Your continued use of the Website constitutes acceptance of the revised policy

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Cookies Policy, please contact us via our Contact Us page or at Denielloenergia@gmail.com or +61 480 806 279.
