About Daniello Energia

Leading the Future of Energy Innovation

Innovating Energy

Daniello Energia is a premier oil and gas servicing company dedicated to providing integrated solutions across the energy value chain. Established in Naples, Italy, and now proudly operating in Brisbane, Australia, we combine European engineering precision with Australian operational excellence. Our team comprises experienced engineers, project managers, and technicians who deliver safe, reliable, and sustainable energy servicing solutions. We take pride in supporting exploration, production, and infrastructure development for both onshore and offshore operations.

Our Mission: To deliver innovative, safe, and efficient oil and gas services that drive global energy development while upholding the highest standards of integrity and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision: To be a globally recognized leader in oil and gas servicing, trusted for our expertise, valued for our quality, and respected for our commitment to sustainability.

Projects & Experience

Delivering Excellence Across Borders

Our experience spans diverse energy environments from offshore operations to inland energy infrastructure. We have proudly partnered with leading energy firms, delivering end-to-end servicing solutions that meet international standards.

Highlighted Projects:

  • Offshore equipment maintenance and rig support in the Gulf region
  • Pipeline construction and commissioning in Queensland, Australia
  • HSE audit and technical training programs for regional operators
  • Supply and logistics management for upstream field operations

Each project reflects our core promise—safety, precision, and reliability.

Sustainability

Committed to Responsible Energy

At Daniello Energia, sustainability is not an afterthought—it’s embedded in everything we do. We are dedicated to protecting the environment, empowering people, and contributing to the long-term success of the energy industry.

Our Sustainability Focus:

  • Reducing carbon footprint through cleaner operational practices
  • Promoting renewable and hybrid energy solutions where feasible
  • Supporting local workforce development and community engagement
  • Enforcing zero-harm principles across all operations
